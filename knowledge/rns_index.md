## Selection Tips

1. **Start with date:** If query involves a specific time period, filter by date first
2. **Keyword search:** For type-specific queries, search filenames for keywords
3. **Cross-reference:** Always consider cross-referencing with CSV (quantitative) or YouTube/Tweets (qualitative)
4. **Official record:** RNS is the most authoritative source for official announcements
5. **Multiple files:** Complex queries may require multiple RNS files (e.g., all Bitcoin purchases)

### "All Bitcoin purchases announced by SWC"
**Selection:** Filter RNS files by "Bitcoin" or "purchase" keyword, date range: April 2025 - February 2026
**Cross-reference:** CSV for quantitative validation

### "What RNS announcements were made in July 2025?"
**Selection:** Filter by date prefix "2025-07" in filename
**Expected:** Bitcoin purchases, share issuances, interim results, trading updates

### "When was the accelerated bookbuild announced?"
**Selection:** Filter by "bookbuild" or "accelerated" keyword
**Cross-reference:** YouTube (2025-07-25 mentions bookbuild), CSV for share count changes
