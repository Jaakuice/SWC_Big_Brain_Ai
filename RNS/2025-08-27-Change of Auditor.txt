27 August 2025

The Smarter Web Company PLC
(“The Smarter Web Company” or "Company")

Change Of Auditor

The Smarter Web Company (AQUIS: SWC | OTCQB: TSWCF | FRA: 3M8), a London listed
technology company, announces that following a competitive tender process, the Board has
appointed PKF Littlejohn LLP as the Company’s independent auditor. Accordingly, Adler Shine
has resigned from its role as auditor with immediate effect and has not raised any matters
that need to be drawn to the attention of the Company’s member or creditors.

Andrew Webley, CEO of The Smarter Web Company said: “I would like to express my thanks
to Adler Shine for their support to date; they have been a pleasure to work with. As we enter
an exciting new phase in the Company’s journey, we have selected PKF Littlejohn as our new
auditors and look forward to working with them.”
