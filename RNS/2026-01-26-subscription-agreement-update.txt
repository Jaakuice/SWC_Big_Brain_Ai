The Smarter Web Company PLC
(“The Smarter Web Company” or the "Company")

Subscription Agreement Update - £0.5m Gross Proceeds

The Smarter Web Company (AQUIS: SWC | OTCQB: TSWCF | FRA: 3M8) announces that
955,000 Ordinary Shares have been placed in accordance with the terms of the Subscription
Agreement announced on 24 December 2025 (the “Subscription Shares”).

The gross proceeds from the placing of the Subscription Shares will be £520,037 (before
expenses), equivalent to approximately £0.54 per share, and the Company will receive
approximately 98.25% of the proceeds as settlement. The balance of Ordinary Shares not yet
placed pursuant to the Subscription Agreement is 59,020,500.
