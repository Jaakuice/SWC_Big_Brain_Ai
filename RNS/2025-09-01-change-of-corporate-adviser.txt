01 September 2025

The Smarter Web Company PLC
(“The Smarter Web Company” or the “Company”)

Change Of Corporate Adviser

The Smarter Web Company (AQUIS: SWC | OTCQB: TSWCF | FRA: 3M8), a London listed
technology company, announces that Strand Hanson Limited has been appointed as the
Company’s AQSE Corporate Adviser with immediate effect.

The Board wishes to record its sincere thanks to Guy Miller and the team at Peterhouse
Capital for their guidance and support. The appointment of Strand Hanson comes as the
Company positions itself for its next stage of development.

Andrew Webley, CEO of The Smarter Web Company said “I would like to thank Guy Miller and
Peterhouse Capital for their support during our first few months of being a listed business. We
are delighted to welcome Strand Hanson as our new Corporate Adviser as we position the
business for its next phase of growth and the exciting opportunities that lie ahead.”
