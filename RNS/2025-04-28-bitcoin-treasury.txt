28 April 2025

The Smarter Web Company PLC
(“The Smarter Web Company” or "The Company")

Bitcoin Treasury

The Smarter Web Company (AQUIS: SWC), a London listed technology company, discloses its
current Bitcoin Treasury.

The total funds raised during 2025 are approximately £2.1m (before expenses).

Current Bitcoin Treasury details are as follows:

• Total Bitcoin Holdings: 2.30 Bitcoin
• Total Average Purchase Price: £73,834 per Bitcoin ($98,216 per Bitcoin)
• Total Amount Purchased: £170,000

Andrew Webley, CEO of The Smarter Web Company said “As we move forward, we aim to
communicate transparently. Investors have asked us to share our current Bitcoin Treasury and
today we have announced this.

As we explore further opportunities through organic growth and corporate acquisitions,
alongside increasing our Digital Assets Treasury, including Bitcoin, we will keep investors
updated.”
