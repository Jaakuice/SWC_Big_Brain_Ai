22 September 2025

The Smarter Web Company PLC
(“The Smarter Web Company” or the "Company")

Subscription Agreement Update - £5m Gross Proceeds

The Smarter Web Company (AQUIS: SWC | OTCQB: TSWCF | FRA: 3M8), a London-listed
technology company and the UK’s largest publicly traded company holding Bitcoin on its
balance sheet, announces that 3,826,799 Ordinary Shares have been placed in accordance
with the terms of the Subscription Agreements announced on 19 June 2025 and 04
September 2025 (the “Subscription Shares”).

The gross proceeds from the placing of the Subscription Shares will be £4,956,905 (before
expenses), equivalent to approximately £1.30 per share, and the Company will receive
approximately 97% of the proceeds as settlement early this week.

All Ordinary Shares pursuant to the Subscription Agreement announced on 19 June 2025 have
now been placed and the balance of Ordinary Shares not yet placed pursuant to the
Subscription Agreement announced on 04 September 2025 is 17,722,000.
