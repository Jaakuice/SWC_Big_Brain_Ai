BOARD OF DIRECTORS

Andrew Webley — CEO & Founder
• Founded The Smarter Web Company in 2009
• Previously Head of Online, Hargreaves Lansdown
• Internet & marketing professional with +25 years' experience
• Involved with Bitcoin since 2017

Sean Wade — Independent Non-Executive Chairman
• CEO, Power Metal PLC
• Has worked on numerous transactions in the capital markets, including IPOs, secondary capital raising and M&A
• Extensive network covering numerous capital providers, including institutional funds, family offices and private wealth

Randal Casson — Senior Independent Non-Executive Director
• Qualified as a chartered accountant with PwC
• Worked with PwC for 35 years, the last 22 years of which he was an audit partner
• A Non-Executive Director at the FTSE 100 company, Games Workshop Group PLC

Martin Thomas — Independent Non-Executive Director
• Qualified solicitor with a 35+ year legal career advising on IPOs and equity/debt offerings in London
• Held senior management positions in law firms, including 7 years as European Managing Partner of a global law firm headquartered in the United States
• Non-Executive Vice Chairman of Diversified Energy Company (NYSE and LSE listed)

Tyler Evans — Non-Executive Director
• Experienced Bitcoin and marketing entrepreneur
• Co-founder and Chief Investment Officer of UTXO Management
• Director of Metaplanet and Nakamoto
• Co-founder of parent company BTC Inc., publisher of Bitcoin Magazine and host of the annual Bitcoin conference
• Involved with Bitcoin since 2013

Note: Albert Soleiman (CFO) departed. Mario Visconti serves as Interim CFO. Oliver Hewett appointed Group Financial Controller (RNS 27 April 2026).
