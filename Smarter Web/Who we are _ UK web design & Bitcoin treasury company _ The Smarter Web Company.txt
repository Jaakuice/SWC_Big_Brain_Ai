A UK based, publicly listed technology company. With a Bitcoin-backed balance sheet.
Building better websites. Powered by digital capital.
Founded in 2009, The Smarter Web Company is a UK-based web design & online marketing business powered by digital capital. We create beautiful websites that help brands grow - and manage a Bitcoin treasury that builds long-term value.
Listed in the UK, we combine creative design with forward-thinking finance to help shape the future of the UK's digital economy.

Our values underpin everything that we do
Clarity — We keep things simple, transparent, and easy to understand.
Performance — Every design, every decision, built for measurable results.
Support — Real people, UK-based, here to help. And to engage.

OUR STORY

2009 — Founded. Bitcoin Genesis Block mined the same year.
2010 — iPad launch drives responsive design demand.
2015 — CMS relaunch for fully responsive design.
2017 — Andrew Webley discovers Bitcoin.
2019 — Remote working model established (pre-pandemic).
2020 — MicroStrategy pivot inspires founder's strategic vision.
2022 — Began accepting Bitcoin payments; searched for UK-listed BTC treasury vehicle.
2024 — Decision to go public.
2025 — Listed on Aquis Exchange at 2.5p. Exceeded £1 billion market cap (21 June 2025). Raised over £225 million in first six months.
2026 — LSE Main Market admission (3 February). Squarebird acquisition announced (23 February).
