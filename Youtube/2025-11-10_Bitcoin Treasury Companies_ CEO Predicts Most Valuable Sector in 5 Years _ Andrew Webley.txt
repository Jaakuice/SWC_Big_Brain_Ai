## Key Topics:
- SWC grew from £1.6M to £200M+ in Bitcoin within 6 months of going public
- Bitcoin treasury companies predicted to become the most valuable sector globally within 5 years
- SWC trades at ~0.8x fully diluted MNAV (~43-45p share price vs ~57-60p MNAV), a 20% discount
- Bitcoin yield strategy: 174% in Q1, ~300% previous quarter; compounding Bitcoin per share justifies premium to NAV
- The "flywheel" effect: equity, ATM, convertibles, debt, preference shares, and options all feed Bitcoin yield
- ATM financing accounts for ~25% of capital raised, operated by a partner above 1x MNAV to protect shareholders
- Bitcoin has historically returned ~30% annually but has underperformed year-to-date (~5%), compressing MNAVs globally
- Momentum buyers who don't understand the long-term model are likely behind selling pressure
- Lending Bitcoin could yield 4-6% annually (~£10M/year), but counterparty risk remains a concern
- Potential acquisitions in web design/marketing sector to substantiate the core business, but only at the right price
