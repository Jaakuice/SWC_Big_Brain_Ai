## Key Topics:
- MicroStrategy's pioneering role in Bitcoin treasury strategy and how it evolved from cash reserves to leveraged capital markets
- MetaPlanet's proof-of-concept that the Bitcoin treasury model works without a legacy software business
- Bitcoin treasury companies as the primary conduit for TradFi capital (fixed income, equities) flowing into Bitcoin
- The UK as the third most important capital market globally for Bitcoin treasury strategy, after the US and Japan
- Bitcoin as "gunpowder" — adopt it or be economically outcompeted by those who do
- SWC's growth potential relative to MetaPlanet; outperforming FTSE companies (averaging ~6%/year) with projected ~30% Bitcoin appreciation
- The FCA regulatory framework and City of London's legacy role in enabling Bitcoin treasury adoption
- SWC's LSE uplisting ceremony — nearly 100 attendees (half shareholders), described as an inflection point for Bitcoin in the UK
- Retail shareholder conviction as the foundational strength of Bitcoin treasury companies, not an afterthought
- The community/network effect around Bitcoin treasury firms and why trust-building with retail investors is non-negotiable
