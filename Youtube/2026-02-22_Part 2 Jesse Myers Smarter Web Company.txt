- The evolution of Bitcoin treasury strategy, from cash reserves to capital market tools, pioneered by Michael Saylor and MicroStrategy

- Bitcoin treasury companies as the primary vehicles for traditional finance (TradFi) capital to flow into Bitcoin over coming decades
- The UK as the second most important capital market in the world and the opportunity for Smarter Web Company to lead Bitcoin treasury strategy there
- The top three capital markets for Bitcoin treasury strategy: US, Japan, and UK
- The role of the FCA and regulatory frameworks in giving London confidence to adopt Bitcoin treasury strategies
- Bitcoin as "gunpowder" — an economic reality you either adopt proactively or get left behind by those who do

- The undervaluation of Bitcoin treasury companies relative to traditional equities when viewed through PE ratio lens
