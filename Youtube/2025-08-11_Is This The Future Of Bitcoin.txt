## Key Topics:
- The mechanics of Bitcoin treasury companies and "accretive dilution" — how issuing equity at a premium to NAV grows Bitcoin per share
- MNAV dynamics and lifecycle: why small, high-growth companies justify higher multiples, and how the P/BID ratio compares to traditional PE ratios
- The UTXO Management thesis: a leading Bitcoin treasury company will emerge in each global capital market
- Trapped capital: how Bitcoin treasury companies serve as conduits unlocking retirement accounts, equity markets, and eventually the $300T+ fixed income market
- Addressing the "Ponzi" critique: why Bitcoin treasury companies differ fundamentally from altcoins, BlockFi, and Celsius
- Smarter Web Company's UK-specific tailwinds: no Bitcoin ETF approved by the FCA, giving SWC access to £1–2 trillion in retirement capital
- Strategy's preferred equity instruments (Strike, Strife, Stride, Stretch): a faster, more flexible evolution beyond convertible notes
- Bitcoin's positively-skewed volatility and why Bitcoin treasury companies "square" that upside volatility
- Why large profitable tech companies (Apple, Microsoft) will be among the *last* to adopt Bitcoin as a treasury strategy
- M&A outlook: consolidation during bear markets, and the race to become the most valuable company in each capital market
