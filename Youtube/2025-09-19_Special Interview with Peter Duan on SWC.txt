## Key Topics:
- Peter Duan's background in traditional finance and journey into Bitcoin via ETF announcements by BlackRock and Fidelity
- How the Bitcoin treasury community size drives mNAV premium and accretive dilution mechanics
- The "four pillars" framework for evaluating Bitcoin treasury companies: CEO communication, a Bitcoin-literate finance bridge, community, and fundraising capability
- Impressions from the NYC Bitcoin Treasury Unconference: bullish but measured sentiment, widespread acknowledgment that fundraising is the key bottleneck
- SWC's growth trajectory: CFO hire, auditor change, marketing hire as signals of LSE uplisting and larger capital market ambitions
- Three levels of Bitcoin treasury companies, from simple corporate treasury to full institutional shareholder base (Bitcoin bank)
- Bitcoin cycles, global liquidity (referencing Lynn Alden and Michael Howell), and caution around extrapolating limited data points
- Strategy's potential S&P 500 inclusion and its mixed implications for SWC shareholders
- Peter's "Bull Standard Academy" — a social media growth platform for Bitcoin companies and content creators
- The importance of patience and selectivity in deals; "waiting for the fat pitch" as the winning strategy in Bitcoin treasury investing
