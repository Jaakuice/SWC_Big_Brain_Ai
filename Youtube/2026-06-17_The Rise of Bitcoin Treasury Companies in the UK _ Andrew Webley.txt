## Key Topics:
- Andrew Webley's personal Bitcoin journey and the inspiration drawn from Michael Saylor's MicroStrategy pivot
- The founding and April 2025 public listing of Smarter Web Company as a Bitcoin treasury company on the London Stock Exchange
- Smarter Web Company's performance: ~3,000 BTC held, £250M raised, 12x fiat return and 60x Bitcoin-per-share growth for shareholders
- The "pure equity" Bitcoin treasury model: raise capital, buy Bitcoin, increase BTC per share, repeat
- The shift from adoption to amplification — using leverage/debt against the Bitcoin balance sheet to enhance returns
- Comparison of Bitcoin treasury companies by amplification ratio and premium (Strive, Strategy, Metaplanet, Smarter Web)
- Bitcoin as the best-performing asset class historically, still representing ~0% of global asset value
- Jesse Myers' model underpinning ~29-30% CAGR projections for Bitcoin over the next two decades
- Smarter Web's ambition to enter the FTSE 250 and FTSE 100, potentially becoming the most valuable company in the UK
- Benefits of Bitcoin treasury companies vs. direct BTC holding or ETFs: compounding BTC-per-share growth over time
