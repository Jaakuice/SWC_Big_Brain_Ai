## Key Topics:
- SWC Bitcoin holdings growth from ~50 BTC to 2,050+ BTC (with cash for 2,300+) in two months
- Stock price volatility from 28p to £6 intraday
- Three capital-raising strategies: private placements, ATM facility, and the new "Smarter Convert" bond
- Smarter Convert structure: 0% interest, 5% conversion premium, 12-month term, worst case = return the Bitcoin
- Meeting with Michael Saylor (Strategy) in London to discuss long-term approach
- Cautious approach to debt despite bullish Bitcoin conviction
- First publicly named institutional investor participating in the Smarter Convert
- NAV reaching approximately £200 million with zero traditional debt
- Apple analogy to simplify the convertible bond mechanics for investors
- "Compound Bitcoin" thesis: why a Bitcoin treasury company can outperform holding BTC directly
