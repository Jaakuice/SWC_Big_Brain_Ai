## Key Topics:
- Bitcoin as a strategic treasury asset for operating companies of all sizes
- Borrowing against Bitcoin (collateralized lending) to access liquidity without selling
- SALT Lending's history and 0% loss rate on over-collateralized Bitcoin loans
- The "one asset, two uses" concept: preserving long-term Bitcoin exposure while funding short-term needs
- Small and medium-sized businesses' lack of access to capital (82% UK SMEs struggle with cash flow)
- Post-pandemic collapse in UK business loan approval rates (from 80% to 37%)
- Reframing debt: secured vs. unsecured credit and why Bitcoin-backed loans differ from traditional debt
- Democratizing sophisticated treasury strategies previously available only to the ultra-wealthy and large corporations
- Bitcoin lending rates declining as the asset class matures and more capital enters the market
- The miner case study: using Bitcoin-backed loans to make payroll, pay power costs, and scale operations
