- Bitcoin as a store of value versus traditional fiat currencies (pound, dollar)
- The origin and mechanics of Bitcoin Treasury Companies, pioneered by MicroStrategy (now Strategy) and Michael Saylor
- The Smarter Web Company's journey from web design agency to the UK's first Bitcoin treasury company

- Skepticism around crypto: the "crypto queen" OneCoin fraud, Sam Bankman-Fried and FTX collapse
- Andrew Webley's career background at Hargreaves Lansdown and founding of The Smarter Web Company
- Bitcoin fundamentals: finite supply, mining, 20-year price history
- The cultural divide on Bitcoin: skeptics, the indifferent, and true believers
- The Satoshi Nakamoto identity mystery and Craig Wright's failed court claim
