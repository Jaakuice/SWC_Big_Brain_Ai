## Key Topics:
- Overview of the Smarter Web Company (SWC) July 2025 investor deck, covering the three strategic pillars: organic growth, acquisitions, and Bitcoin treasury
- SWC's Bitcoin treasury strategy modelled after MicroStrategy/Strategy, becoming the largest UK public company holding Bitcoin on its balance sheet
- Accretive dilution explained: how issuing shares at a premium to net asset value increases Bitcoin-per-share even as share count grows
- mNAV (market cap to net asset value multiple) tracking and what drives Bitcoin treasury companies to trade at a premium
- Organic growth: SWC's web agency business with 250 clients, high gross margins, 6-year average client lifespan, and pricing transparency
- Acquisition strategy: targeting businesses with 3–4 year payback periods using a mix of stock and cash, with concerns raised about whether returns beat Bitcoin's hurdle rate
- Capital markets roadmap: milestones of 1,000 → 2,100 → 21,000 BTC and the evolving instruments (ATMs, book builds, convertibles, preferred equity) used at each stage
- Key team members: Andrew Webley (CEO, ex-Hargreaves Lansdown), Jesse Myers (Bitcoin strategy advisor, Stanford MBA), Tyler Evans (MetaPlanet director, UTXO Management co-founder)
- The broader legitimacy Bitcoin treasury companies bring to Bitcoin in the UK public markets
- How early-stage the UK Bitcoin treasury space still is, with most mainstream investors yet to discover SWC
