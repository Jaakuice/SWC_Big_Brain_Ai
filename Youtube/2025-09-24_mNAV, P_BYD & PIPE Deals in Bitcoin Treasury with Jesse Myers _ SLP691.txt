## Key Topics:
- Smarter Web Company overview: 2,500+ BTC held, ~1.6x mNAV, 278% Q3 Bitcoin yield
- Why most Bitcoin treasury companies have failed to deliver consistent Bitcoin yield
- PIPE deals: how discounted private placements create structural headwinds via hedge fund shorting and misaligned investors
- The Metaplanet playbook: start small, build track record, grow retail base, then attract institutional capital
- Winner-takes-most dynamics in each capital market jurisdiction (Strategy in US, Metaplanet in Japan, SWC targeting UK)
- Long-term potential: Bitcoin treasury companies could accumulate up to half of all Bitcoin over 20 years, channelling fixed income capital into BTC
- Why an mNAV premium is justified: Bitcoin treasury companies are productive assets compounding BTC-per-share over time
- The P/BYD (Price to Bitcoin Yield Delivered) ratio as a new valuation metric analogous to P/E ratio
- Retail investor base as a critical foundation for the Bitcoin treasury flywheel
- M&A consolidation among Bitcoin treasury companies: Strive/Semler as early example, with more expected
