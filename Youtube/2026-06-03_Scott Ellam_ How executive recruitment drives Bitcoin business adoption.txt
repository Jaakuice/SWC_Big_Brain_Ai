## Key Topics:
- XCE (Connecting Excellence Group) is a publicly listed executive recruitment business with an integrated Bitcoin treasury
- Scott Ellam's Bitcoin discovery in 2016 and the three key properties: 21 million hard cap, unconfiscatability, and transportability
- The COVID lockdown catalyst: money supply doubling threatened the business's cash reserves, prompting a Bitcoin balance sheet commitment in October 2021
- How executive recruitment works: recruiters are the core asset, billing hundreds of thousands annually but receiving no equity or long-term incentives
- Three structural problems facing private recruitment company owners: cash debasement, scaling headcount, and lack of exit options
- XCE's solution: Bitcoin treasury for cash preservation, performance-based equity for recruiter attraction/retention, and a listed vehicle for acquisition exits
- The compounding flywheel model: more recruiters → stronger operating business → more credible Bitcoin treasury → more attractive to investors → more recruiters
- Massive addressable market: thousands of privately owned recruitment firms globally with 5–50 headcount and no exit or equity options
- Bitcoin is most powerful when integrated into people-driven, service-based operating businesses
- Executive recruiters speak confidentially with business leaders in every industry and jurisdiction — giving XCE a unique channel to drive global Bitcoin corporate adoption
