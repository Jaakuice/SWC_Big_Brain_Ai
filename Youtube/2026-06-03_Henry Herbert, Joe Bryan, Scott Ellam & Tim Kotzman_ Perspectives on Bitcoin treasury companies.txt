## Key Topics:
- Bitcoin vs Bitcoin treasury companies: fundamentally different assets (money vs equity), but complementary in a portfolio
- UK trapped capital (£1.5–2 trillion in ISAs and SIPPs) driving demand for Bitcoin treasury companies as an access vehicle
- Smarter Web Company's IPO as a catalyst for UK Bitcoin treasury adoption and community building
- The challenge of explaining Bitcoin treasury valuations: why they should trade above or below NAV
- Not all Bitcoin treasury companies are equal: leadership conviction, long-term commitment, and business model matter
- Operating businesses with integrated Bitcoin treasuries vs pure-play treasury shells: the scaling tension
- Bitcoin treasuries as an onboarding vehicle for people who lack confidence in self-custody
- The Cantillon effect and how large treasury companies can "weaponize the financial system against itself"
- Bear market as the ultimate test of treasury company conviction — those who hold (or accumulate) will emerge stronger
- Communicating Bitcoin to friends and family: shifting from zealot advocacy to practical pension conversations
