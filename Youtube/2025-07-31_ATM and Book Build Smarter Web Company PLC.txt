## Key Topics:
- What a book build is and how it works as an equity fundraising method
- How Smarter Web Company PLC uses book builds to raise funds for Bitcoin purchases
- Book build pricing history: June 26th at £2.90, July 18th at £2.95 — never below previous prices
- Why institutions prefer book builds over open market purchases (avoiding slippage)
- What a direct placement is and how it differs from a book build
- Disadvantages of book builds: lumpy execution, lagging intraday premiums, MNAV dislocation
- What an ATM (At-The-Market) mechanism is and how it works
- Smarter Web Company's special ATM rules: no selling on down days, only 20% of volume, only above previous closing price
- How ATMs dampen volatility and keep share price aligned with MNAV
- Comparison of conventional ATMs vs. Smarter Web Company's rule-based ATM structure
