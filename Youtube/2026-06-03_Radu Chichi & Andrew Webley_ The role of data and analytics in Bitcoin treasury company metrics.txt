## Key Topics:
- Radu's background: Amazon engineer who invested his signing bonus in Bitcoin in early 2017 and fell down the rabbit hole
- Origins of StrategyTracker (formerly MSDR Tracker / Sailor Tracker) as a tool to bridge the information gap between Bitcoin believers and the wider world
- The power of analytics as concentrated storytelling — combining "number goes up" with institutional endorsement to communicate Bitcoin's value
- The need for Bitcoin treasury companies to align on a common narrative and standardized metrics (e.g., mNAV, Bitcoin yield) to reduce investor confusion
- Challenges of understanding Bitcoin treasury companies: requires first understanding Bitcoin across computer science, economics, engineering, and more
- Bitcoin yield must be viewed alongside leverage/amplification to give a complete picture
- The case for industry-wide data standardization: common formats, definitions, and structures shared with analytics providers
- Smarter Web Company's efforts to work with other companies on standardizing data output
- Bitcoin price outlook: Radu is short-term bearish (potential drop to $50K) but extremely bullish over 10 years; Andrew focuses on consistent accumulation regardless of short-term price
- HODL as the key principle: uncertainty in the short term makes steady accumulation the most reliable strategy
