## Key Topics:
- Introduction to Smarter Web Company, a UK-listed Bitcoin treasury company on the Aquis exchange
- The company's strategy of mirroring MicroStrategy (now Strategy) by acquiring Bitcoin on its balance sheet
- Explosive share price performance: 12,400% gain from listing at 4p to a peak of £5, followed by an 80% crash
- MNAV (multiple of net asset value) as the key metric for evaluating Bitcoin treasury companies
- Why investors buy Bitcoin treasury stocks instead of holding Bitcoin directly (simplicity, regulation, ISA/pension eligibility)
- The Bitcoin power law model and its price predictions ($1M by 2030–2035)
- The economic model: borrowing fiat currency cheaply to buy an appreciating asset
- Skepticism around valuing Bitcoin with no cash flows and reliance on "greater fool" dynamics
- Founder Andrew Webbley's ambitious goal to acquire 1% of all Bitcoin (210,000 BTC)
