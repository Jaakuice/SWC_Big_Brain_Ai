## Key Topics:
- Introduction to Smarter Web Company as the UK's first Bitcoin treasury company, modelled on MicroStrategy/Strategy
- SWC's extreme share price volatility: 12,400% rise from 4p to £5, then 80% crash from peak
- mNAV (multiple of net asset value) explained as the key metric for valuing Bitcoin treasury companies
- Why investors buy Bitcoin treasury shares instead of Bitcoin directly (ISA/pension access, regulation, simplicity)
- Bitcoin Power Law as the mathematical model underpinning Bitcoin treasury investment theses
- Debate over Ponzi scheme characteristics vs. legitimate (if reckless) business model
- Bitcoin vs. gold as a store of value — gold's millennial track record vs. Bitcoin's technological vulnerability
- Phase 1 (accumulate Bitcoin) vs. Phase 2 (become the new banks) of the Bitcoin treasury company strategy
- Government intervention risks: historical gold confiscation precedent and potential Bitcoin nationalisation
- Cyber security and custodial risks (e.g. Coinbase hack scenario), and broader risks of technology-based assets being superseded
